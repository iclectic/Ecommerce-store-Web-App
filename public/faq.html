Frequently Asked Questions

Does the television come with a warranty?

Yes, all our televisions come with a standard manufacturer warranty. The duration of the warranty may vary depending on the brand and model.

Can I wall-mount the television?

Absolutely! Most of our televisions are wall-mountable. Make sure to check the product specifications or consult our customer support for guidance on wall-mounting compatibility.

What are the available screen sizes?

We offer a wide range of screen sizes to suit various preferences. Our televisions are available in sizes ranging from 32 inches to 85 inches and even larger in select models.

What are the available connectivity options?

Our televisions come with a variety of connectivity options, including HDMI, USB, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and more. You can connect your devices, gaming consoles, streaming devices, and other peripherals effortlessly.

Is the television compatible with smart features?

Yes, many of our televisions are equipped with smart features, allowing you to access popular streaming services, browse the internet, and enjoy a wide range of apps directly on the TV.

Can I purchase additional accessories for the television?

Absolutely! We offer a wide range of accessories, including wall mounts, soundbars, cables, and more, to enhance your television viewing experience. Check out our accessories section for more details.

What payment options are available?

We offer various payment options, including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Choose the most convenient method for you during the checkout process.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide international shipping to several countries. During the checkout process, you can select your country for delivery. Please note that additional customs duties or taxes may apply based on your location.

What is your return policy?

We have a hassle-free return policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return the television within 30 days for a refund or exchange. Please refer to our Returns and Refunds page for detailed information.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment on our website or the shipping carrier's website.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via phone or email. Visit our Contact Us page for more information, including our contact details and working hours.

Are the prices inclusive of taxes?

Yes, the prices listed on our website include applicable taxes. You won't have to worry about any additional taxes during checkout.
